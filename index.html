WEB DEVLOPMENT

Internet presence using website or portals is the one essential tool in the modern business world which you cannot do without. Every business needs to project their profile or get their solution, content and data the exposure needed. This is absolutely essential in getting and retaining customers. Orikid team can help you reach your target market in the web. Our expertise is in the followingareas:

– Servers: Node.js, Appache, Tomcat, AWS and Azure
– Languages: Javascript (client, server), Php, Python
– Frameworks: Express, Mean stack, Heroku stack, AWS & others
– Databases: MongoDB, MySQL, Oracle and others
– Front End: JavaScript, HTML5, Css, JQuery, AngularJS.

DIGITAL MARKETING

A team of dedicated Digital Marketing (SEO/SEM) consultants provide clients with the kind of PR and web presence that they need. Our team works with the clients to come up with the best possible solution through a multi-pronged strategy utilizing all the modern tools of the internet and smart keywords to ensure that our client’s application and business get the viewership and the necessary traction with their clientele.

MOBILE APP DEVLOPMENT

Mobile applications represent the new cutting edge in marketing and communications for a business. There are hundreds of millions of smart phones and other devices with internet access or SMS. Businesses today understand the potential reach to customer and are now looking for solutions along these lines. Orikid team has right expertise to help your business go mobile through an application that best represents your business. Our expertise is in the following technologies and services:

– iPhone, Android and Hybrid Apps
– SMS/Text based applications
– VOIP based Apps
– Web services (REST/Json) for your data or content
